What is Kabbalistic Numerology?

Kabbalistic numerology (Gematria) is the mystical interpretation of Hebrew letters and words based on their numerical values. Each Hebrew letter carries a specific value, and words with matching totals are believed to be interconnected spiritually.

This system is used in Kabbalah to uncover deeper meanings in the Torah, enhance meditation practices, and explore the divine.
