What is Kabbalistic Numerology?

Kabbalistic numerology (Gematria) is the mystical interpretation of Hebrew letters and words based on their numerical values. Each Hebrew letter has a specific value, and words with the same values are thought to have hidden spiritual connections.

This system is used in Kabbalah to uncover deeper meanings in the Torah, enhance meditation, and explore the divine.
